Alerts

Provide contextual feedback messages for typical user actions.

Primary: This is a neutral informative message.
Success: Your changes have been saved.
Warning: Please check your input.
Error: Something went wrong.

Toast

Floating notifications that appear at the corner of the screen.

Toast notifications are ephemeral messages that disappear after a short duration.

The text content of the toast is logged to the console for debugging purposes.

Toast notifications are considered a subset of alerts and can be styled using .toast + .alert + .success, .warning, or .error.
